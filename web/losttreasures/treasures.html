1. Lost Dutchman Gold Mine

Located in Arizona, this yet to be discovered treasure supposedly lies within a cave in the Superstition Mountains. People have been trying to find it since the 1840's.


2. Forest Fenn's Treasure

A man named Forest Fenn decided to hide a chest filled with treasure when he was diagnosed with cancer. After he left several clues, thousands have searched the Rockies, yet all have returned empty handed.


3. The Flor do Mar

A massive Portugese ship that could be called the Titanic of its day, suffered a similar fate. On it's journey back with a fortune on board, it sank. The wreck has yet to be found.


4. Montezuma's Treasure

Even though the Spanish conquistadors defeated the Aztecs, they still didn't manage to get all their treasure. A man named Freddy Crystal met a descendant of Montezuma (or so the legend goes) that gave him information about this seemingly hidden stash of gold and jewels. Along with some townspeople, he searched the caves but ended up just finding booby traps. Maybe the treasure has already been found or it probably never existed.
