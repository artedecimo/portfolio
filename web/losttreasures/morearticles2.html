Not just any fisher, Mel Fisher, a Keys local who is popular with beachgoers nearby. He lives near a marina and he's constantly on the lookout for whatever washes up on the beach.

During his weekly scuba dive, Mel discovered some rotting wood near a reef offshore. Inspecting it closely, he discovered similar pieces around the area. Searching along with his friends they eventually came to an old ship.

Not wanting to disturb it more than needed, they took some pictures and showed it to other treasure afficionados. The fate of the wreck is yet to be determined.
