Freelance Web Design Agreement

Client/Freelancer Relationship

Freelancer is working as an independent contractor for client, not an employee.

Web Hosting

All web hosting and domain accounts have a separate third-party subscription with a domain/hosting provider. Client may choose to setup their own domain/hosting service, or Freelancer may setup domain and/or hosting for Client, which would then be billed as a maintenance expense.

Project Timeline

The project timeline is determined by the Client contributing to the workflow with prompt responses, as well as the Freelancer receiving content for the website in a timely manner. Any delays in approvals or responses from the Client will significantly affect the projected timeline, with the possibility of the original deadline being postponed. By agreeing to these terms, Client acknowledges their responsibility in keeping the project on-schedule. On the other hand, if the Freelancer is unable to meet project deadlines, the project timeline and subsequent payments will be adjusted accordingly.

Maintenance

Maintenance done during the project timeline will be billed at the completion of the project unless noted otherwise. Any payment for maintenance done after the completion of the project, will be due on a weekly basis at the standard hourly rate.

Third-party services or subscriptions

Invoices for subscriptions (ex: stock photography) and/or additional materials and services authorized by Client for the project are due before said subscriptions/materials are purchased. Third-party Subscriptions/services will continue to be billed to Client on a recurring basis (if applicable) until termination of project, or until Client determines that subscription/service will be canceled, whichever is sooner.

Custom Assets

At the request of Client, Freelancer may create custom graphics including but not limited to (animated GIFS, graphics, etc). Payment for these customized assets are due before assets are created.

Ownership

Freelancer retains ownership of all custom assets, custom web templates, and all other ("original content") created for Client by Freelancer. Upon completion of project and final payment, Client will be given a license to use and display the ("original content") with no attribution required.

Browser-Compatibility

The Web browsers accounted for are the most recent versions of: Firefox, Safari, Chrome, and Internet Explorer.

E-Commerce Laws

Client is solely responsible for complying with appropriate laws or taxes relating to their use of an e-commerce website. Freelancer is not responsible for any costs, fees, suits, claims, penalties or otherwise, related to Client's use of their e-commerce website.

Payments

Work will begin once the 1st payment is recieved. Payments will only be processed thru Paypal. Even if you do not have a Paypal account, you will have the option to pay by credit or debit card thru Paypal at checkout.

Invoices

All invoices are due as soon as they are recieved. If Freelancer doesn't recieve payment within 7 days of the invoice date, it will be assumed, at Freelancer's own discretion, that the Client terminated the project.

Termination

Client or Freelancer may terminate this agreement at anytime by giving prior written notice (by email) to the other party. If Client terminates this agreement, all prior work done by Freelancer up until the termination date will be billed to Client. Once Agreement is terminated, Freelancer will no longer be responsible for providing additional services to Client.

If the agreement is terminated because of Freelancer's inability to complete the project, the amount of a refund given to Client shall be determined based on hours spent and/or how close the project was to completion.

Severability

If any term of this Agreement is to any extent illegal, otherwise invalid, or incapable of being enforced, such term shall be excluded to the extent of such invalidity or unenforceability; all other terms hereof shall remain in full force and effect; and, to the extent permitted and possible, the invalid or unenforceable term shall be deemed replaced by a term that is valid and enforceable and that comes closest to expressing the intention of such invalid or unenforceable term.

Questions

Ideally we all want things to work out perfectly, but if there are any questions or concerns, please send a message so that we can figure something out.

Thanks for your cooperation!
