Web Design Agreement

  • Client/Web Designer Relationship

    • Web Designer is working as an independent contractor for Client, not an employee.
    • Web Designer is only specialized in Front-End Web Design. Client shall seek other resources regarding Back-End programming or scripting.

  • Web Hosting

    • All web hosting and domain accounts have a separate third-party subscription with a domain/hosting provider.
    • Client may choose to setup their own domain/hosting service, or Web Designer may setup domain and/or hosting for Client, which would then be billed as a maintenance expense.

  • Project Timeline

    • The project timeline is determined by the Client contributing to the workflow and delivering content to Web Designer in a timely manner. Any delays in approvals or responses from the Client will significantly affect the projected timeline, with the possibility of the original deadline being postponed.
    • By agreeing to these terms, Client acknowledges that Web Designer shall determine how many revisions will be made without affecting the project schedule. On the other hand, if the Web Designer is unable to meet project deadlines, the project timeline and subsequent payments will be adjusted accordingly.

  • Maintenance

    • Maintenance done during the project timeline will be billed at the completion of the project unless noted otherwise. Any payment for maintenance done after the completion of the project, will be due on a weekly basis at the standard hourly rate.

  • Third-party services or subscriptions

    • Invoices for subscriptions (ex: stock photography) and/or additional materials and services authorized by Client for the project are due before said subscriptions/materials are purchased.
    • Third-party Subscriptions/services will continue to be billed to Client on a recurring basis (if applicable) until termination of project, or until Client determines that subscription/service will be canceled, whichever is sooner.

  • Custom Assets

    • At the request of Client, Web Designer may create custom graphics including but not limited to (animated GIFS, graphics, etc). Payment for these customized assets are due before assets are created.

  • Ownership

    • Web Designer retains ownership of all custom assets, custom web templates, and all other ("original content") created for Client by Web Designer.
    • Upon completion of project and final payment, Client will be given a license to use and display the ("original content") with no attribution required.

  • Browser-Compatibility

    • The Web browsers accounted for are the most recent versions of:
      • Firefox
      • Safari
      • Chrome
      • Internet Explorer .

  • E-Commerce Laws

    • Client is solely responsible for complying with appropriate laws or taxes relating to their use of an e-commerce website.
    • Web Designer is not responsible for any costs, fees, suits, claims, penalties or otherwise, related to Client's use of their e-commerce website.

  • Payments

    • Work begins once Initial Payment is recieved. Subsequent payments will be invoiced. Payments will only be processed thru Paypal .
    • All invoices are due as soon as they are recieved. If Web Designer doesn't recieve payment within 7 days of the invoice date, it will be assumed, at Web Designer's own discretion, that Client terminated the project.

  • Termination

    • If Client terminates the project, all prior work done by Web Designer up until the termination date will be billed to Client. Once project is terminated, Web Designer will no longer be responsible for providing additional services to Client.
    • If the project is terminated because of Web Designer's inability to complete their tasks, the amount of a refund given to Client shall be determined based on hours spent and/or how close the project was to completion.

  • Severability

    • If any term of this Agreement is to any extent illegal, otherwise invalid, or incapable of being enforced, such term shall be excluded to the extent of such invalidity or unenforceability; all other terms hereof shall remain in full force and effect; and, to the extent permitted and possible, the invalid or unenforceable term shall be deemed replaced by a term that is valid and enforceable and that comes closest to expressing the intention of such invalid or unenforceable term.

  • Questions

    • Ideally we all want things to work out perfectly, but if there are any questions or concerns, please send a message so that we can figure something out. Please only contact and expect a response during normal weekday working hours.
    • Thanks for your cooperation!
