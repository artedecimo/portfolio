Web Design Agreement

  • Client/Web Designer Relationship

    • Web Designer is working as an independent contractor for Client, not an employee.

  • Web Hosting

    • All web hosting and domain accounts have a separate third-party subscription with a domain/hosting provider.
    • Client may choose to setup their own domain/hosting service, or Web Designer may setup domain and/or hosting for Client, which would then be billed as a maintenance expense.

  • Project Timeline

    • The project timeline is determined by the Client contributing to the workflow with prompt responses, as well as the Web Designer receiving content for the website in a timely manner.Any delays in approvals or responses from the Client will significantly affect the projected timeline, with the possibility of the original deadline being postponed.
    • By agreeing to these terms, Client acknowledges their responsibility in keeping the project on-schedule. On the other hand, if the Web Designer is unable to meet project deadlines, the project timeline and subsequent payments will be adjusted accordingly.

  • Maintenance

    • Maintenance done during the project timeline will be billed at the completion of the project unless noted otherwise. Any payment for maintenance done after the completion of the project, will be due on a weekly basis at the standard hourly rate.

  • Third-party services or subscriptions

    • Invoices for subscriptions (ex: stock photography) and/or additional materials and services authorized by Client for the project are due before said subscriptions/materials are purchased.
    • Third-party Subscriptions/services will continue to be billed to Client on a recurring basis (if applicable) until termination of project, or until Client determines that subscription/service will be canceled, whichever is sooner.

  • Custom Assets

    • At the request of Client, Web Designer may create custom graphics including but not limited to (animated GIFS, graphics, etc). Payment for these customized assets are due before assets are created.

  • Ownership

    • Web Designer retains ownership of all custom assets, custom web templates, and all other ("original content") created for Client by Web Designer.
    • Upon completion of project and final payment, Client will be given a license to use and display the ("original content") with no attribution required.

  • Browser-Compatibility

    • The Web browsers accounted for are the most recent versions of:
      • Firefox
      • Safari
      • Chrome
      • Internet Explorer .

  • E-Commerce Laws

    • Client is solely responsible for complying with appropriate laws or taxes relating to their use of an e-commerce website.
    • Web Designer is not responsible for any costs, fees, suits, claims, penalties or otherwise, related to Client's use of their e-commerce website.

  • Payments

    • Work will begin once the 1st payment is recieved. Payments will only be processed thru Paypal.
    • Even if you do not have a Paypal account, you will have the option to pay with credit or debit card thru Paypal at checkout.

  • Invoices

    • All invoices are due as soon as they are recieved. If Web Designer doesn't recieve payment within 7 days of the invoice date, it will be assumed, at Web Designer's own discretion, that the Client terminated the project.

  • Termination

    • If Client terminates this agreement, all prior work done by Web Designer up until the termination date will be billed to Client. Once Agreement is terminated, Web Designer will no longer be responsible for providing additional services to Client.
    • If the agreement is terminated because of Web Designer's inability to complete the project, the amount of a refund given to Client shall be determined based on hours spent and/or how close the project was to completion.

  • Questions
