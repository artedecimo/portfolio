Freelance Web Design Agreement

Web Hosting

All web hosting and domain accounts have a separate third-party contract with a domain/hosting provider. Client may choose to setup their own hosting service, OR Freelancer may setup domain and/or hosting for client, which would then be billed at the standard hourly rate.

Project Timeline

The project timeline is determined on the client contributing to the workflow with prompt responses, as well as recieving content for the website in a timely manner. Any delays in approvals or responses from the client will significantly affect the projected timeline, with the possibility of the original deadline being postponed. By agreeing to these terms, client acknowledge their responsibility in keeping the project on-schedule. On the other hand, if the Freelancer is unable to meet project deadlines, the project timeline and subsequent payments will be adjusted accordingly.

Maintenance

Maintenance done during the project timeline will be billed at the completion of the project unless noted otherwise. Any payment for maintenance done after the completion of the project, will be due on a weekly basis at the standard hourly rate.

Third-party services or subscriptions

Invoices for subscriptions (ex: stock photography) and/or additional materials and services authorized for the project are due before said subscriptions/materials are purchased. Third-party Subscriptions/services will continue to be billed on a recurring basis (if applicable) until completion of project, or until client determines that subscription/service will no longer be used.

Custom Assets

At the request of client, Freelancer may create custom graphics including but not limited to (animated GIFS, graphics, etc). Payment for these customized assets are due before assets are created.

Ownership

Freelancer retains ownership of all custom assets, custom web templates ("original content") created for Client by Freelancer. Upon completion of project and final payment, client will be given a license to use and display the ("original content") with no attribution required.

Browser-Compatibility

The Web browsers accounted for are the most recent versions of: Firefox, Safari, Chrome, and Internet Explorer.
